How to Find Your Input Values

This guide explains what each input means and how you can find your values.

1. Age

Enter your current age in years.

2. Sex

1 for Male, 0 for Female.

3. Chest Pain Type (CPT)

Select the type of chest pain:
1 - Typical Angina, 2 - Atypical Angina, 3 - Non-Anginal Pain, 4 - Asymptomatic

4. Resting Blood Pressure (RBP)

Measured in mmHg. Normal range: 90-140.

5. Cholesterol Level

Enter your serum cholesterol in mg/dL.

6. Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)

1 if > 120 mg/dL, otherwise 0.

7. Resting Electrocardiographic (RECG)

0 - Normal, 1 - ST-T wave abnormality, 2 - Left ventricular hypertrophy.

8. Maximum Heart Rate (MaxHR)

Your maximum heart rate achieved during exercise.

9. Exercise-Induced Angina (EA)

1 if yes, 0 if no.

10. Oldpeak

ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.

11. ST Slope

1 - Upsloping, 2 - Flat, 3 - Downsloping.
